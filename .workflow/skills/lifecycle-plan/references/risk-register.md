# Risk Register

Every meaningful plan needs an explicit risk register.

Required fields:

- risk
- likelihood
- impact
- mitigation
- owner or owning phase
- affected manifest IDs
- waiver needed: yes/no

Risk categories to consider:

- source-of-truth ambiguity
- domain or policy ambiguity
- public contract or compatibility change
- generated-output drift
- release, rollback, or handoff risk
- verification gap
- protected path or ownership conflict
- unrelated user changes overlapping planned work

Risks without mitigation must block plan approval or require a documented user waiver.
